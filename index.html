How your candy kills orangutan

One ingredient in most of your favirote treats is palm oil.
Palm oil is exsracted from palm trees the way your candy kills orangtauns is it's habitat is destoryed just to plant palm trees which leaves them homeless.
Palm oil is a huge cash crop that candies like skittles, Almond Joy, and Hersey's contain.
The reason palm oil is so valbuble is because of it's texture, shelf life, Taste, Cost, and Stability.


Ways you can save orangtauns habitats

  1. Supporting NGOs and Advocacy Groups:Funding and Support: Provide financial and logistical support to NGOs and advocacy groups that are working on the ground to protect orangutan habitats and combat deforestation.
  2. Supply Chain Management: Push for companies to improve their supply chain management to avoid sourcing palm oil from areas that contribute to deforestation.
  3. Corporate Responsibility: Encourage companies to commit to zero deforestation policies and to source 100% certified sustainable palm oil.

Things that DON't help orangtauns

  1. Failing to Educate and Raise Awareness: Avoid overlooking the importance of educating younger generations on environmental issues and conservation.
  2. Disregarding Deforestation-free Alternatives: Using Non-Traceable Palm Oil: Don't use palm oil that cannot be traced back to a sustainable source. This often means it comes from areas where deforestation and habitat destruction are prevalent.
  3. Underestimating the Importance of Policy and Advocacy: Don't underestimate the power of advocacy. Ignoring the need to pressure governments and corporations can hinder significant progress.
